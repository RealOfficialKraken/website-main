MattBookPro - About Me

A Brief Introduction

Heyo! My name is Matthew Krueger, I was born on June 18th in Columbus, Ohio in 1999. My older sibling Joey was 5 at the time, excited to see he finally had a little brother. Our family had always moved around considering my father's career in Film Production. So when I was 2, we moved to Oceanside, California. We jumped around the southern side like Menifee, Temecula, and Murrieta. Throughout the years, I got a little brother, Joshua. Then came Emily, my first and only little sister. And finally, Adam, the youngest brother, was born into our family.

My favorite music is melodic, any genre that pulls this off, pulls me in.

My favorite take-out food is In-N-Out Burger, and my favorite dish is Chicken Risotto, and/or a Medium Rare Porterhouse.

My favorite types of video games are puzzle/over-arching stories.

My favorite story narratives are meta types

I am exceptional at competing in Rocket League, and at my peak, I was the top 0.02% of players on the ranked leaderboard. Grand Champion was my highest rank at 1613 MMR

I think the best expression of myself can be exhibited through the art that I do. I love making things. The concept of Head to Tangibility has been a huge interest for me for all of my life.

Yes, I am related to a "Fredrick Adolf Krueger", my Grandfather on my dad's side. Contrary to popular belief, he's actually a Christian pastor who loves and prays for everyone whom he meets. No joke!
