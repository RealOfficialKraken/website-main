I was inspired by most of the big artists in House Music. I love making beats the way David Guetta, Avicii, Galantis, and Swedish House Mafia. They would all take apart their own melodies into it's core components. If that alone sounded good, like for example, singing with a piano or guitar, imagine what the final studio product would sound. This collection is a resemblance of all of that, I truly think it's the best way to make melodic music. And my favorite way of expressing that is through House Music, as there are so many more effects you can play around to build tension. One day I hope to play some of these tracks in it's final form at a big set.
