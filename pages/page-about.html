MattBookPro - About Me

A Brief Introduction

Heyo! My name is Matthew Krueger, I was born on June 18th in Columbus, Ohio in 1999. My older sibling Joey was 5 at the time, excited to see he finally had a little brother. Our family had always moved around considering my father's career in Film Production. So when I was 2, we moved to Oceanside, California. We jumped around the southern side like Menifee, Temecula, and Murrieta throughout the years.

My favorite take-out food is In-N-Out Burger, and my favorite dish is Chicken Risotto, and/or a Medium Rare Porterhouse.

My favorite types of video games are puzzle/over-arching stories.

I am exceptional at competing in Rocket League, and at my peak, I was the top 0.02% of players on the ranked leaderboard. Grand Champion was my highest rank at 1613 MMR

That's me, in the blue shirt. I've always had an over-active imagination, constantly creating things in the gargantuan mindscape I call my head. My mother, on the other hand, called it "Matthew's World". In 2002, my family bought us a Playstation 2, and my older brother and I couldn't be more excited. My very first video game title, Jak and Daxter, was a completely revolutionary game at the time. The game still holds near and dear to my heart, to this day (see here for more details). I believe Joey said I ended up beating it when I was 5 years old.
